# Zudio-sales-performance-dashboard

✨ Zudio Sales Performance Dashboard | Data Analysis Project
Developed an interactive sales performance dashboard to visualize and analyze key business metrics for Zudio(2024), providing actionable insights into customer behavior, regional performance, and operational efficiency.

📊 Key Insights
Revenue & Volume: Tracked total sales of 25.05M and a total revenue of 63M across 36K units sold.
Regional Performance: Identified West Bengal as the top-performing state (3.24M), followed closely by Delhi and Rajasthan, indicating strong market penetration in these regions.
Customer Base: Managed data for over 7.8K customers, with a specialized view of the Top 10 High-Value Customers to support loyalty targeting.
Category Distribution: Analyzed sales across Kids, Men, and Women segments, noting a near-even split in revenue contribution (approx. 33% per category).
Operational Factors: Correlated sales performance with store attributes like Store Type (Owned vs. Rented) and Parking Availability to understand environmental impacts on footfall.


📍 Tools Used
Data Visualization : Power BI (or Tableau)
Data Cleaning : Excel 
Data Handling : SQL(Basic)

💡 Key Recommendations
Inventory Optimization: Maintain high stock levels in West Bengal and Delhi to capitalize on peak demand and prevent stockouts.
Customer Retention: Implement a personalized rewards program for the identified "Top 10" customers to increase lifetime value (LTV).
Strategic Expansion: Focus future store openings on "Owned" models with "Available Parking," as these factors appear to be tracked as significant performance drivers.
Seasonal Marketing: Address the sales dip observed in June and December with targeted seasonal campaigns to stabilize monthly revenue.
